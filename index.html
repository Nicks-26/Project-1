Featured Lawyer

Alexander Nichole Pacar

Alexander Nichole Pacar is the Founding Partner and Managing Partner of Pacar and Associates.

He has almost five years of litigation experience, handling various civil, commercial, administrative, and criminal cases. His practice area also covers contracts, construction arbitration, and agrarian reform.

He has successfully handled the litigation of a variety of court and administrative cases in both the regular courts and quasi-judicial bodies, and acts as counsel for a wide range of clients from individual magnates to institutional corporate clients.
